About Pinnacle Shield Insurance

Pinnacle Shield Insurance was founded in 1985 and has grown into a trusted provider of auto, home, and life insurance. Headquartered in Hartford, Connecticut, we proudly serve customers across all 50 states.

Our mission is to make insurance simple, transparent, and accessible. We focus on personalized service, fast quotes, and dependable coverage for every stage of life.

At Pinnacle Shield Insurance, we believe that every family deserves peace of mind. That is why we work hard to provide flexible coverage options that fit different needs, lifestyles, and budgets.
